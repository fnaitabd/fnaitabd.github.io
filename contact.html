Areas of research and interests

My research interests lie in the field of computer and communication networks with emphasis on architectures and protocols for quality of service and security in wireless and wired networks. Areas of current interest include future Internet, vehicular networks, wireless sensor networks, Internet of things and healthcare networked systems, and network security.
